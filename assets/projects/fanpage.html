Who is Tower of Power?

Founded by Emilio Castillo in 1968, this is the grandfather of West Coast R&B. Where many bands have come and gone, Tower of Power continues to delight audiences with their unique blend of jazzy big band sound and funky soul grooves. 2018 sees the band on a US tour to celebrate 50 years making music together.
